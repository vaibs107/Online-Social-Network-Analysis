Data is collected for 5 different football teams. The number of people they follow is determined and based on the common friends the graph is ploted. 
Further the betweenness is calculated and graph is partitioned using Girvan Newman Alogirthm to find 2 clusters.

For classification, separate data is collected for the top 4 businessmen of USA. Their recent tweets are collected and using affin, the total positive and negative scores are calculated.
Finally, depending on the total scores the document is classified as positive or negative.
